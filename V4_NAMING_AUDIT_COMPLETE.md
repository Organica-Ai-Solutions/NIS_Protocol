## 💡 Recommendations for Future Phases

### **Naming Guidelines**:
1. ✅ Be literal about capabilities
2. ✅ Avoid buzzwords (quantum, blockchain, etc.) unless actually using them
3. ✅ Add clarification notes for anything that might be misinterpreted
4. ✅ Use metaphorical names (like "Genesis") but explain the metaphor
5. ✅ Always state what you're NOT doing if there's potential confusion

### **Examples of Good Names**:
- ✅ "Multi-Path Reasoning" (describes what it does)
- ✅ "Agent Genesis" (metaphor but clear)
